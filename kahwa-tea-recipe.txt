Ingredients:
 - 2 cups water
 - 8 cardamom pods
 - 8 cloves
 - 1 cinnamon stick
 - 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
 - 2 tablespoons kahwa green tea leaves
 - 4 crushed almonds
 - 4 dried rose petals
 - dash of saffron

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, boil 2 cups of water.
  2. Bring to a simmer and add cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, sugar and tea leaves.
  3. Heat on low for 15 - 20 minutes.
  4. Strain into cup and garnish with saffron, almonds and rose petals.
